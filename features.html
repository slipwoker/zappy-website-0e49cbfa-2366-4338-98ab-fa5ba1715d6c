How It Works

Your Pass to Savings Starts Here

The HBCU Yard Pass is a mobile-friendly digital savings platform built for the entire HBCU ecosystem — from enrollment to everyday redemption, it's effortless.

From Enrollment to Everyday Savings — Instantly.

Once enrolled, members receive immediate access to the HBCU Yard Pass interactive web portal — a mobile-friendly savings platform designed to work seamlessly from your smartphone, tablet, or computer.

Browse and search thousands of discounts and offers available locally and nationwide. Simply log in, find participating merchants or categories, and begin using available savings instantly. No waiting, no complicated steps — just real value for the HBCU community, right when you need it.

Whether you're heading to Homecoming, traveling to a football classic, dining with friends, or shopping online, the HBCU Yard Pass puts your savings to work everywhere you go.
