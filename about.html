Our Mission

Where Legacy Meets
Everyday Empowerment

The HBCU Yard Pass was built to honor what HBCUs have always represented — excellence, community, and the power of shared purpose. We exist to merge cultural pride, lifestyle access, and financial empowerment into one shared membership experience, giving every student, alumnus, faculty member, and family a way to live the legacy while building real economic strength.

From Homecoming weekend to everyday essentials, from football classics to family vacations, the Yard Pass connects the entire HBCU ecosystem to tens of thousands of participating merchants nationwide. It's not just a savings platform — it's a community investment tool that funds scholarships, supports HBCU institutions, and celebrates the culture that defines us.

Honoring legacy. Delivering value. Building community — one pass at a time.

Beyond Savings

A Community Investment Tool

The HBCU Yard Pass is more than a discount platform — it is a living, breathing investment in HBCU institutions, scholarship pathways, and the cultural legacy that defines our community. Every membership, every merchant interaction, every saved dollar flows back into the ecosystem that built us.

01

Support Institutions

When HBCU Yard Pass members save at participating merchants nationwide, a portion of that economic activity is channeled back to support the very institutions that shaped our community. HBCUs have long operated with fewer resources than their peer institutions — the Yard Pass helps close that gap by creating a continuous, community-driven funding stream. From infrastructure upgrades to academic program support, your membership helps keep the lights on, the doors open, and the legacy growing. This is driveline retail merchandising reimagined for the HBCU ecosystem — turning everyday purchases into institutional power.

Institutional Empowerment
02

Strengthen Scholarship Pathways

Access to education should never be a privilege reserved for the few. The HBCU Yard Pass is committed to strengthening scholarship pathways so that the next generation of HBCU graduates can walk across that stage without the crushing weight of financial barriers. A portion of program revenues is directed toward scholarship initiatives, merit awards, and emergency student funds at participating HBCU campuses. Students, alumni, faculty, and families who carry the Yard Pass are not just saving — they are actively funding the dreams of future scholars who will carry this legacy forward.

Promote Culture and Leadership

Homecoming weekends, football classics, step shows, Greek-letter pageantry, and the bonds formed on the yard are traditions worth preserving and amplifying.
