What Is HBCU Yard Pass?

More Than a Membership.
A Digital Experience Built for the HBCU Community.

The Official HBCU Yard Pass is an interactive web-based savings and lifestyle portal designed specifically for students, alumni, faculty, staff, families, supporters, and organizations across the entire HBCU ecosystem. It connects every member of the community to a powerful nationwide savings network — providing access to exclusive discounts and benefits at tens of thousands of participating businesses across America.

More than just a membership card, the HBCU Yard Pass is a digital platform created to celebrate HBCU culture while helping members save money on the products, services, experiences, and everyday purchases they already enjoy. Whether attending Homecoming, traveling to football classics, dining with friends, or planning family vacations — your pass works wherever you go.

Culture-Connected Savings

Every discount, deal, and benefit is curated with HBCU pride at its core — from Homecoming weekends and Greek organization events to everyday retail, dining, and travel savings that resonate with your lifestyle.

Digital Access Anywhere

Once enrolled, members receive immediate access to the HBCU Yard Pass interactive web portal and mobile-friendly savings platform. Log in, browse thousands of participating merchants, and start saving instantly — no physical card required.

Pride with Practical Value

The program combines school pride, community investment, lifestyle benefits, and financial savings into one seamless membership experience — supporting HBCU institutions, scholarships, and cultural initiatives while delivering real everyday value.
